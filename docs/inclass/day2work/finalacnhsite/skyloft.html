SKYLOFT

the hidden gem of the northern hemisphere!

named for the outcropping of land sent into the sky by the goddess hylia that would protect and house the hylians, skyloft is truly a tiny utopia. it may not be a predecessor of hyrule, but is its own paradise all the same. in 2020, entrepreneur and mogul tom nook, alongside land developer lucy matteson, set out for the then-deserted island in search of new beginnings. they found that the resource-rich land, spotted with pansies and peach trees, would make a lovely home for villagers across the land. their determination and resourcefulness, and a few helpful flights from orville, turned skyloft into the mecca it is today.
